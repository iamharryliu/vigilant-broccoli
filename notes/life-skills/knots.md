# Knots

| Knot                                                                                            | Usage                                                                                 | Image                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Square Knot](https://www.animatedknots.com/square-knot)                                        | Joining two ends of a rope around an object, e.g. tying bundles or packages.          | ![Square Knot](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a0/Square_knot.svg/500px-Square_knot.svg.png)                                                                                                     |
| [Clove Hitch](https://www.animatedknots.com/clove-hitch-knot-rope-end)                          | Quick attachment of a rope to a post or pole; easy to adjust but can slip under load. | ![Clove Hitch](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1f/Clove_Hitch_-_ABoK_11_-_USCG.jpg/500px-Clove_Hitch_-_ABoK_11_-_USCG.jpg)                                                                       |
| [Sheet Bend](https://www.animatedknots.com/sheet-bend-knot)                                     | Joining two ropes, especially of different thicknesses.                               | ![Sheet Bend](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0d/Schotstek_rechts.jpg/500px-Schotstek_rechts.jpg)                                                                                                |
| [Bowline](https://www.animatedknots.com/bowline-knot)                                           | Fixed loop at the end of a rope that won't slip or bind; easy to untie after load.    | ![Bowline](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/Palstek_innen.jpg/500px-Palstek_innen.jpg)                                                                                                         |
| [Figure 8 Loop](https://www.animatedknots.com/figure-8-follow-through-loop-knot)                | Strong, secure fixed loop; standard tie-in knot for climbing.                         | ![Figure 8 Loop](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/65/FigureOfEightLoop.jpg)                                                                                                                             |
| [Round Turn + Two Half Hitches](https://www.animatedknots.com/round-turn-two-half-hitches-knot) | Securing a rope to a post, ring, or tree; holds well under constant load.             | ![Round Turn + Two Half Hitches](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/ff/Anderthalb_Rundt%C3%B6rn_mit_zwei_halben_Schl%C3%A4gen.jpg/500px-Anderthalb_Rundt%C3%B6rn_mit_zwei_halben_Schl%C3%A4gen.jpg) |
| [Taut Line Hitch](https://www.netknots.com/rope_knots/tautline-hitch)                           | Adjustable loop that holds under tension; tensioning tent guy lines.                  | ![Taut Line Hitch](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Topsegelschotstek.jpg/500px-Topsegelschotstek.jpg)                                                                                         |
