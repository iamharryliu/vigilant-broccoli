## Eggs

### Poached Eggs

- strain egg
- simmering water with splash of vinegar
- light swirl and lightly drop egg in water
- boil for 3 mins and poke to check if it's done

## Soft Boiled Eggs

- splash of vinegar
- boil for 6 minutes
- remove and run under cold water

## Hard Boiled Eggs

- splash of vinegar
- boil for 7 minutes
- remove and run under cold water
