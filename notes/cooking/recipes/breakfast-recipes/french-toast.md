# French Toast

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, beaten
- 240ml milk
- 120ml sugar
- 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (I add a bit extra)
- 120ml butter
- slices of bread

## Instructions

- Blend ingredients to coat bread and pan fry.
- Serve with fresh fruit like sliced banana or berries.
- Optional toppings: maple syrup.
