# Coconut Chia Pudding

## Ingredients

- **Pudding Base**
  - 1 x 400ml can light coconut milk
  - 5 tbsp chia seeds
  - 1 tbsp honey (or maple syrup for vegan option)
  - Â½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  - Tiny pinch kosher salt

- **Toppings (optional)**
  - Fresh fruit (e.g., blueberries, raspberries)
  - Jam
  - Nuts

## Instructions

- In a medium mixing bowl, stir together the coconut milk, chia seeds, honey (or maple syrup), vanilla, and salt until **well combined**.
- Cover and refrigerate overnight.
- In the morning, stir again to ensure even distribution of chia seeds.
- Adjust sweetness with additional honey or maple syrup if desired.
- Portion into bowls and **add toppings of choice**.
