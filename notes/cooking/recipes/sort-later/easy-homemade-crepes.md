# Easy Homemade Crepes

## Ingredients

- Batter
  - 125g all-purpose flour
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 2 large eggs, **at room temperature**
  - 240ml milk, **warmed**
  - 45g melted butter
  - 60ml water
- Cooking
  - 30ml cooking oil, for the skillet

## Instructions

- In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt, eggs, milk, melted butter, and water until a smooth batter forms.
- **Let the batter rest at room temperature for 30 minutes** or refrigerate for up to two days.
- Heat a 25cm non-stick skillet over medium heat and brush with **about 1/2 tsp oil**.
- Pour **about 90g of the batter** into the center and tilt the skillet to cover the surface.
- Cook until mostly set, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
- Stack cooked crepes on a plate and cover with a towel to keep warm.
- Fill, fold, or roll the crepes to serve.
