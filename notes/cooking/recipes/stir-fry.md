# Stir Fry Recipes

## Recipe 1

### Ingredients

- Tofu
  - Extra-firm tofu block
  - ½ tsp kosher salt
  - ½ tsp white pepper
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - Heaping 1/4 tsp Chinese five spice powder (optional but amazing!)
  - 3 tbsp cornstarch or arrowroot powder
- Sauce
  - 1 ½ tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 ½ tbsp rice vinegar
  - 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  - 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  - 1 tbsp organic brown sugar (see Note 3)
  - 1 to 2 tbsp chili-garlic sauce (or sambal oelek)
  - 3 tbsp veggie broth or water
- Aromatics & Vegetables
  - 2 inch piece ginger finely chopped
  - 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  - 14 to 16 oz vegetables of choice
- Finishing
  - Cornstarch slurry - 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp cold water
  - 2 tbsp black sesame seeds or toasted white sesame seeds
  - 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  - Slices green onions.

## Recipe 2

### Ingredients

- Whatever vegetables you want
- Sauce - soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and honey in 1:2:1:1 ratio

### Instructions

- Cut vegetables into shapes so that they will blanch evenly.
- Blanch vegetables.
- Add oil to pan and a few cloves of garlic.
- Add sauce.
- Cook on high until moisture at the bottom has left.
