# Lemon Yakult

## Ingredients

- Half a lemon
- 1 tbsp of honey
- 240ml water
- Ice cubes
- Yakult

## Instructions

- Squeeze half a lemon to extract the juice.
- In a glass, combine lemon juice, honey, and water.
- Stir well until honey is dissolved.
- Add ice cubes.
- Pour in yakult and stir gently.
- Serve immediately.
