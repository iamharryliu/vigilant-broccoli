# Ginger Tea

## Ingredients

- Water
- Ginger
- Cinnamon stick
- Lemon
- Honey

## Instructions

- Slice fresh ginger into thin rounds.
- Bring water to a boil in a pot.
- Add sliced ginger and cinnamon stick.
- Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
- Strain into a cup.
- Add honey and lemon juice to taste.
