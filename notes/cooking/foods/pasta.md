# Pasta

## Basics

Pasta

- Boil pasta to al dente.
- Remove and cool pasta in cold water.

Sauce

- Heat fat source such as butter or a cooking oil on pan.
- Add diced onions and garlic.
- Add crushed chili peppers.
- Add cream.

Mix.
