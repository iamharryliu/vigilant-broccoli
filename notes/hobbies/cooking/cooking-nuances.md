# Cooking Nuances

| Nuance                                | Difference                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **White miso vs dark miso**           | White miso is mildly sweet, lightly fermented, and delicate. Dark miso is stronger, saltier, and aged much longer for deeper umami.                                                                                       |
| **Light soy sauce vs dark soy sauce** | Light soy sauce is thinner, saltier, and primarily used for seasoning. Dark soy sauce is thicker, slightly sweeter, less salty, and mainly used to add rich color and depth.                                              |
| **Mirin vs rice wine (sake)**         | Mirin is a sweet Japanese cooking wine with a high sugar content that adds sweetness and gloss to dishes. Rice wine (sake) is much drier and less sweet, contributing subtle flavor without adding significant sweetness. |
