# Kitchen Knife Management

## Equipment

- [Whetstone](https://www.google.com/search?q=whetstone)
- [Honing Steel](https://www.google.com/search?q=honing+steel)

## Routine

- Use honing steel regularly during use and between use of knives.
- Use whetstone sharpening when the knife is too dull
