# Bok Choy Stir-fry

## Ingredients

- bok choy
- neutral oil
- garlic
- salt
- msg
- chicken buillon powder or vegetable buillon powder + yeast

## Instructions

- Cut and clean bok choy.
- Heat up pan with oil on hot and add garlic for 15 seconds before adding bok choy.
- Stir fry until bokchoy stem has a nice green color.
- Add salt, msg, garlic buillon powder, and chicken buillon powder / vegetable buillon powder + yeast and stir fry for another minute.
