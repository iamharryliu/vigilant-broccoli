# Nerano

## Ingredients

- zucchini
- olive oil
- pasta
- basil leaves
- parmesan

## Instructions

- Boil pasta.
- Fry zucchini in olive oil until crisped.
- Strain zucchini and oil into container and place zucchinis on paper towels to dry.
- Put most of the zucchini back on the pan with some olive oil leaving the rest of the zucchini to top off the pasta later.
- Add pasta to the pan along with basil leaves.
- Mix grated parmesan and pasta water to make paste.
- Cook on pasta in pan and keep adding water until the pasta is to liking.
- Add paste to pasta and toss
- Top with parmesan, basil leaves and extra zucchini.
