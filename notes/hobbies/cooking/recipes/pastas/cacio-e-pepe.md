# Cacio e Pepe

## Ingredients

- paste
- salt
- pepper
- pecorino romano
- lemon zest

## Instructions

- Boil pasta
- Add pasta water to pan and add pepper
- Mix grate lemon zest, grated pecorino romano, andground pepper into a bowl with some paste water to make a paste.
- Add pasta to pan and cook in pan adding pasta water until to liking.
- Add pasta to bowl with the paste that we mixed earlier. Stir and toss around to coat the pasta evenly.
- Top off with lemon zest, grated cheese, and pepper to liking.
