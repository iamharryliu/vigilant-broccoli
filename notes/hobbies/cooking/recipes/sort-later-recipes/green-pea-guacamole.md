# Green Pea Guacamole

## Ingredients

- Peas
  - 300g frozen green peas, thawed
- Dressing
  - 1 tsp crushed garlic
  - 60ml fresh lime juice
  - ½ tsp ground cumin
- Mix-ins
  - 1 tomato, chopped
  - 4 green onions, chopped
  - 125g chopped fresh cilantro
  - 0.5ml hot sauce
- To taste
  - Sea salt

## Instructions

- **Blend** the peas, garlic, lime juice, and cumin in a food processor until smooth.
- Transfer the mixture into a bowl and **stir in** the tomato, green onion, cilantro, and hot sauce.
- **Add salt** to taste.
- **Cover** and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
