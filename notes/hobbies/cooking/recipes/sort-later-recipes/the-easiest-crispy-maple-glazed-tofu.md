# The Easiest Crispy Maple Glazed Tofu

## Ingredients

- Tofu
  - 397g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
- Cooking Oil
  - 30ml neutral cooking oil (avocado, vegetable, canola, or refined coconut oil)
- Maple Glaze
  - 30ml soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  - 30ml maple syrup
  - 15ml rice vinegar
- Coating
  - 15g cornstarch
  - 5g garlic powder
  - 2.5g smoked paprika
  - 2.5g salt
  - Pinch of black pepper
- Garnish]
  - Toasted sesame seeds
  - Chopped green onions

## Instructions

- **Press the tofu** for at least 30 minutes to remove excess moisture. Pat dry, cut into 1.25cm cubes, or diagonally slice into triangles.
- In a large bowl, mix cornstarch, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper, then toss in the tofu until evenly coated.
- Heat neutral oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat until warmed through. Add tofu in a single layer and cook for about 10 minutes, or until **golden and crispy** on all sides.
- While the tofu is cooking, whisk together soy sauce, maple syrup, and rice vinegar in a small mixing bowl. Set aside.
- Once the tofu is golden, reduce the heat to low and pour the maple glaze over the tofu. Stir constantly for 1-2 minutes, or until **the glaze thickens** and evenly coats the tofu.
- **Serve immediately** while warm and crispy, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions, as desired.
