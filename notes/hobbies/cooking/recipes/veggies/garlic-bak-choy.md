## Ingredients
- Bak-choy
- Sauce:
  - 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp Sugar
  - 1 tsp chicken bouillon powder
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1 tsp corn starch
  - ¼ cup water Water
- 8 cloves minches garlic

## Instructions
- Blanch  bak choy for 1 min in salted water.
- Sauté garlic in pan.
- Add sauce.
- Add blanched bak choy.
