# Chinese Scrambled Eggs

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tomatoes
- salt
- sugar
- MSG
- white pepper
- fish sauce
- cornstarch slurry
- knobs of butter
- green onion

## Instructions

- Mix eggs, salt, sugar, MSG, white pepper, fish sauce, half the cornstarch slurry and knobs of butter.
- Cook scrambled eggs and remove from pan.
- Fry green onion in pan and add tomatoes.
- Add salt, sugar, ketchup and stir.
- Add the rest of the cornstarch slurry.
- Return eggs to pan.
- Serve with rice.
