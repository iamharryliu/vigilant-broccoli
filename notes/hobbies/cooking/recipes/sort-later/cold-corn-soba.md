# Cold Corn Soba

## Ingredients

- Soba Noodles
- Corn Stock
  - 3 corn ear cobs (kernels removed)
  - korean anchovy stock tablet or hondashi powder
  - kombu
  - 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp mirin
  - 2 cups water
  - salt to taste
- creamy corn sauce
  - blanched corn kernels
  - silken tofu (1/3 weight of kernels)
  - corn stock (1/3 weight of kernels)
  - salt to taste
