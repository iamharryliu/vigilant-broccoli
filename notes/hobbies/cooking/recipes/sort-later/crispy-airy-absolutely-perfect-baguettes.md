# Crispy, Airy, Absolutely Perfect Baguettes

## Ingredients

- Dough
  - 220 ml Water
  - 3 g Fresh yeast (or 2 g dry yeast)
  - 320 g Bread flour
  - 6 g Salt

## Instructions

- **Mix**: Dissolve yeast in water. Add flour and salt, mix until combined (no kneading needed).
- **First rest**: Cover, rest 30 minutes.
- **Stretch and fold**: With wet hands, stretch 4 sides and fold to center. Lift and slap dough back into bowl.
- **Second rest**: Cover, rest 30 minutes.
- **Repeat**: Stretch and fold 4 sides again. Lift and slap once more.
- **Main rise**: Let double in size, about 2 hours.
- **Divide**: Turn onto floured surface, divide into 3 equal parts.
- **Pre-shape**: Gently flatten each piece, roll, and seal seam. Rest uncovered 10 minutes.
- **Final shape**: Flatten, roll into baguette shape, seal edge, taper ends.
- **Final proof**: Transfer to baking tray with parchment dividers. Cover with towel, rise 30 minutes.
- **Bake**: Preheat oven to 220Â°C. Dust with flour, score deeply with blade. Sprinkle water around (not on) baguettes, cover with another tray. Bake 20 minutes covered, then 10 minutes uncovered until golden.
