# Easy Silken Tofu

## Ingredients

- Tofu
  - 1 package silken tofu
- Sauce
  - 37.5ml soy sauce
  - 15ml black rice vinegar
  - 5ml toasted sesame oil
  - 15ml chili oil crisp
  - 5ml sugar
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 5ml toasted sesame seeds
- Garnish
  - 2 green onions, diced
  - 15g cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

- **Drain the tofu** from the package and pat dry with paper towels.
- **Slice the tofu** into 1.27cm slices and place on a serving plate.
- In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, black rice vinegar, sesame oil, chili oil, sugar, sesame seeds, and minced garlic.
- Drizzle the sauce over the tofu.
- **Garnish with green onions and cilantro**. Enjoy!
