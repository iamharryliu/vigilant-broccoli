# Easy Silken Tofu

## Ingredients

- Tofu
  - 1 package silken tofu
- Sauce
  - 37.5ml soy sauce
  - 15ml black rice vinegar
  - 5ml toasted sesame oil
  - 15ml chili oil crisp
  - 5g sugar
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 5g toasted sesame seeds
- Garnish
  - 2 green onions, diced
  - 15g cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

- **Drain** the tofu from the package and place it on a paper towel-lined cutting board.
- **Pat** the tofu to dry excess moisture and slice it into 1.25cm slices, then place them on a serving plate.
- In a small mixing bowl, **combine** soy sauce, black rice vinegar, sesame oil, chili oil, sugar, sesame seeds, and minced garlic.
- **Drizzle** the sauce over the tofu and garnish with green onions and cilantro.
- **Serve cold** and enjoy!
