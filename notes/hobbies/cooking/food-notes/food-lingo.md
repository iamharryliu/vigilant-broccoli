## General Cooking Terms

| Term      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Deglazing | The process of pouring liquid (wine, broth, or water) into a hot pan to loosen and dissolve the browned, caramelized bits of food stuck to the bottom. Creates a flavorful sauce or gravy. |
| Quorn     | Fungus-based meat alternative made from mycoprotein. High in protein and low in fat, commonly used as a substitute for meat in vegetarian and vegan cooking.                               |
