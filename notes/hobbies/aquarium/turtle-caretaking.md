# Turtle Caretaking

## Feeding

- Active eating time is from late spring into early fall. He only needs to eat food that is roughly the same size as his head every other day.
- Add food to water, turtles can only eat food in water.

## Aquarium Maintenance

### Cleaning Filter Media

1.  Turn off the power on the black power strip.
2.  Remove filter media basket and put in an empty bucket.
3.  Remove some aquarium water and add to another bucket.
4.  Remove filter media (sponge, filter floss, bio media) from basket and rinse in the bucket of aquarium water.
5.  Return filter media to filter media basket (sponge, filter floss, bio media).
6.  Return filter media basket.
7.  Turn back on power.

### Water Change and Refilling Water

- Scoop out pitchers of water into a bucket and pour out water into sink/toilet.
- You can refill water either by using tap water (requires water conditioner) or using rain water (does not require water conditioner).
- Refill via rainwater:
  - To refill using rain water just scoop pitchers of rain water from a bucket into the aquarium
- Refill via tap water:
  - Fill up a large bucket of water and add a capful of water conditioner.
- Refill the aquarium water to a height that dexter is able to comfortably enter his basking dock.
