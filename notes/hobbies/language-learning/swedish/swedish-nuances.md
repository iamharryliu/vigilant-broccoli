# Swedish Nuances

- **många vs mycket**
  - många when you can count
  - mycket when you cannot count and next to verbs ie regnar mycket
  - pengar(money) is an exception to the counting rule
- apartments
  - 1a - 2 bedroom
  - 2a - 3 bedroom
  - 3a - 4 bedroom
- **till** vs **på** vs **i**
- det kommer
