## Guidelines

- Trim songs to same volume before mixing in.
- Don't play the low bass of two songs at once.
  - Increasing highs mids of song 2 in while gradually decreasing lows of song 1

| Term                 | Description |
| :------------------- | :---------- |
| Beats                | Individual rhythmic pulses in music, typically counted as kick drum hits (1, 2, 3, 4) |
| Bars                 | Groups of beats, usually 4 beats per bar in most dance music |
| Phrases              | Musical sections made of multiple bars, typically 8, 16, or 32 bars that form complete musical ideas |
| Beat Matching        | Aligning the beats of two songs so they play in sync without drift |
| Tempo Matching       | Adjusting BPM of tracks to match each other using pitch/tempo controls |
| Phase Alignment      | Fine-tuning the timing so beats hit at exactly the same moment (using jog wheel or phase sync) |
| Sync Button          | Automatic feature that matches tempo and phase of tracks instantly |
| Frequency Management | EQ mixing technique to control which frequencies (lows, mids, highs) from each track are playing |
| Trim                 | Gain control to normalize volume levels between tracks before mixing |
